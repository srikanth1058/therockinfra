2&3BHK Apartments In Tellapur | Project By The Rock Infra In Collaboration With Tripura Constructions

Ruminate & Unwind your thoughts in the Galaxy of The Rock Infra Own a spacious and well-ventilated apartment @Tripura Galaxy to suit your modern lifestyle

As the name proclaims these are spacious 2 & 3 BHK apartments @ Tellapur which is just a few minute’s drives from Hi-Tech City. The Rock Infra is built to live an elegant lifestyle with 107 apartments in a stilt + 5 upper floors. These flats are built on an area of 1430 sft and 1210 sft. Each flat is equipped with modern facilities and is provided with well-ventilating spaces.

This project is close to Gachibowli and Nanakramguda. It pockets the state-of-the-art amenities such as round clock –security and water supply, excellent electricity and drainage facilities etc. The complex also offers entertainment facilities like gym, clubhouse, jogging track, community hall and playing spaces.

Tellapur has become a sought after destination for the professionals working in a number of international corporate and IT giants in Gachibowli. This place is well connected to IT companies, educational institutions, hospitals, malls and various entertainment spots. Galaxy has well developed physical and social infrastructure. Own an apartment at Galaxy and enjoy your life in the serene and tranquil atmosphere of Tellapur.
